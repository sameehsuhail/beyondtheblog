Those of you who haven't been completely educated about mensuration openly heres a bit of free sex education class for y'all so menstruation or period is normal vaginal bleeding that occurs as part of a woman's monthly cycle, this is a very important process fall fertilizing to take place. So, every month the uterus of a woman prepares for pregnancy. If fertilization does not occur the uterus or the womb sheds its lining along with a few blood vessels now the menstrual blood is partly blood and partly tissue from the inside of the uterus and it passes out to the vagina.

Periods usually start between ages 11 to 14 or it varies in women sometimes and can continue until menopause status are at about 50 to 51 years of age.
