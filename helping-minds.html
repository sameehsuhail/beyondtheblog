Mental Health Toolkit

I feel depressed

1. talk to someone
2. get moving
3. make a new hobby
4. add mindfulness to your day
5. express gratitude
6. get your daily dose of sunlight
7. don't ignore suicidal thoughts
8. find a simple source of joy
9. reframe your negative thoughts
I feel lonely

1. find creative ways to stay in touch
2. help others
3. distract yourself
4. remember this isn't forever, it'll pass
I feel stir crazy

1. remember why staying at home is important
2. structure your day
3. try meditation
